# Contributing & Paid Bounties

Thanks for your interest! You can contribute or do small paid tasks.

How to contribute
- Open a new issue describing the change or bug.
- If you want to work on it, comment `I will work on this` and link your PR.

Paid bounties
- We label paid issues with `bounty` and list the payment amount in the issue body.
- Before starting: get written confirmation in the issue or WhatsApp (scope, price, delivery time).
- After completion: create a PR, request review, and mark the issue `ready for payment`.
- Payment methods: Paystack / Payoneer / Crypto. We will pay after review/acceptance.

Issue templates
- Please follow the `bug report` or `feature request` template when opening issues.
